Giada Palmieri

PhD Candidate
g.palmieri[at]uu.nl


I’m a PhD candidate at the Institute for Language Sciences , Utrecht University.

I received my BA in Foreign Languages and Literature from the University of Bologna in 2016 and my MA in Linguistics from Utrecht University in 2018.
That same year, I joined as a PhD candidate Yoad Winter’s ERC project ‘Forests and Trees: the Formal Semantics of Collective Categorization’, where I’ve been focusing on reciprocal predicates.
My research interests include semantics, verbal morpho-syntax, plurality-related phenomena and - more broadly - argument structure, (lexical) semantic universals, linguistic fieldwork and the interaction between formal linguistics and typology, and how they can support one another.

My first name is pronounced ['dʒa:da], or ['ʒa:ða] in my native Central Italian variety. My pronouns are she/her.
